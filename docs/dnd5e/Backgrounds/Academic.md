#Backgrounds
### Academics
Academics use their education and knowledge from extensive studies to make a living and achieve their lifeâ€™s ambitions. Academics are generally held in higher regard than most other backgrounds, as their skills are sought after by the wealthy and powerful. Academics make great researchers, investigators, and versatile problem-solvers.

**Skill Proficiencies:** Choose one from Arcana, History, Investigation, Medicine, Nature, Religion.
**Languages:** Choose one standard and one exotic.
**Equipment:** A bottle of black ink, a quill, a letter opener, parchment, and 10 gp.
